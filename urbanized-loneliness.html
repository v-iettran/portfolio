Urbanized Loneliness

The idea of the series is inspired by the function of photography to trigger the nostalgic feeling. At the same time, I am curious about the state of loneliness that each of us is going through. “Ways of Seeing” of John Berger also alters the way I capture the photos in order to get the meaning I want to send. The main idea these photos try to convey is to highlight the loneliness of the people living in the industrialization era. Shanghai is a perfect destination since it is a modern and convenient city. This allows people in the city to live more comfortable but simultaneously more separated because of the high pace living. The photo order goes from the broad view to the details of the city. Each of the photo will have a different feeling that related to the keyword “loneliness”. Through the series, I want people to engage the loneliness with their understanding and have their own conclusion about the definition of “the urbanized loneliness”.
